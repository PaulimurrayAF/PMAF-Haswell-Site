About the Pauli Murray Awards Foundation

Honoring Black LGBTQ+ brilliance through celebration, community, and collective power.

Our Mission

The Pauli Murray Awards Foundation (PMAF) uplifts, invests in, and celebrates Black LGBTQ+ individuals across the U.S. through recognition, resources, and leadership development. Inspired by the legacy of Rev. Dr. Pauli Murray, we honor the powerful intersections of race, gender, sexuality, and spirituality.

Our Vision

We envision a world where Black LGBTQ+ people are fully seen, valued, and resourced to lead. Through annual awards, mentorship programs, and visibility campaigns, we aim to create a future rooted in equity, legacy, and joy.
