Our Values

Legacy

We honor those who paved the way and those shaping history now.

Liberation

We root our work in the unapologetic freedom of Black LGBTQ+ people.

Visibility

We celebrate and amplify our communities in all their nuance and brilliance.

Community

We build across generations, identities, and geography with love and purpose.

Honoring Black LGBTQ+ brilliance across generations and disciplines. The Pauli Murray Awards Foundation uplifts legacy, liberation, visibility, and community.
